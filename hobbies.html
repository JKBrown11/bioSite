Reading

Wayne enjoys reading science fiction/fantasy. Some of his favorites are the Hitch-Hiker's Guide to the Galaxy, the Eragon Series, and anything by Brandon Sanderson.

Video Games

Wayne was asked what his all time favorite video game was, as he's been playing since childhood. He responded with Fable for original XBox or Sid Meiers Pirates for the original NES. While he holds a soft spot for the classics, he currently plays a lot of Modern Warfare 3 online, to keep in touch with friends spread across the US.

Family Time

While life can be busy with work and/or school, Wayne prioritzes hanging out with his family. From bonfires and smores, to waterballon fights, he strives to keep his family laughing. The family loves trying and/or making new pizza together, playing with dogs, and playing Minecraft.
