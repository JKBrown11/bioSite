Military Service

Wayne considers his military service a cornerstone of his current life. His absence in his hometown for the initial training left a gap in many hearts. Countless letters to and from home helped remind him that his conditions were temporary. After initial training, he proposed to a former roommate, and his previous life joined his new. His time in the Navy taught him many things about electronics, server maintenance, and radar systems. Much of the training he received carries over to general security principles he currently employs elsewhere.

Education

As mentioned in the short biography, Wayne is a current student under the GI Bill pursuing a master's degree in cyber security. His ultimate goal is to be a penetration tester, but there are several more certificates and years of experience required to meet that goal. As he progresses into a highly skilled field, he is hopeful that he will earn opportunities to better his life.

Professional Gamer

If all of life's troubles didn't require steady income to provide food and shelter, Wayne might choose a more laid back career. He has often stated that if he could make legitimate money playing video games, he would. As technology continues grow, and platforms like Twitch and YouTube continue to be popular, the idea isn't terribly far-fetched. However, dad-jokes (good or bad) may limit his audience.
