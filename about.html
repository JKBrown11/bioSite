About Wayne

Wayne Brown is a married man with one child. He also happens to be my husband. He enjoys playing video games, tabletop games, reading, and watching tv. He is excellent at dad jokes and troubleshooting. He is from the same hometown as me.

He was in the US Navy for 6 years before returning 'home' to the Southeastern US. His family started shortly after entering the Navy and grew to include one more during that time. Since then, he has obtained a bachelor's degree in information technology, and will have a master's in cyber security before 2024 year-end. All his post military education was paid for by a benefit of military service known as the Montgomery GI Bill.

Currently for work, Wayne installs and maintains security systems for local business chains in the state. This includes security cameras, safe repair, and lock box unlocking. When he is not busy with work and school, he enjoys spending time with his family or enjoying a nice hobby.

Wayne's current hobbies include online gaming, reading, and watching tv. If he had an unlimited budget for hobbies, he would likely have a metal forge and a wood shop. One of his favorite books of all time is The Hitch-hiker's Guide to The Galaxy by Douglas Adams. One of his favorite tv shows is Doctor Who from BBC America.

Overall, Wayne is an excellent husband, father, and a well-rounded human. He enjoys making people laugh and playing with puppies.
